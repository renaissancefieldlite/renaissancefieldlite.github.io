Full medical capture surface

Not one wearable. A hospital-facing signal layer.

  • First live lanes - MoFit HRV/RR and Muse Athena EEG/optical/IMU prove the capture surface.
  • Clinical vitals expansion - ECG, PPG/SpO2, blood pressure, respiration, sleep, temperature, posture, gait, tremor, and EDA/GSR.
  • Metabolic and chemistry lanes - CGM/glucose, lactate, hydration/electrolytes, cortisol, sweat patches, and skin temperature.
  • Hospital interface - normalized windows, signal quality, device context, state vectors, specialist views, and FHIR-ready exports.
Auxiliary fork Muse Athena AI Session Analysis can become a focused product lane for EEG/HRV session summaries, tuning notes, and export packets, but B.A.S.I.S. remains the larger medical infrastructure surface.
