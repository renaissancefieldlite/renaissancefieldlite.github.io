Installer packages

Choose your operating system.

Mac

Mac AU/VST3 package

For macOS 12 Monterey or newer, Apple Silicon or Intel. Includes AU and VST3 plugin formats for compatible DAW hosts.

Download Mac package

Install into the standard macOS Audio Plug-Ins folders, then rescan plugins in your DAW if needed. Look under VST3 or Audio Units > RenaissanceFieldLite > StellaCordis.

PC

Windows PC VST3 package

For Windows 10 or Windows 11, 64-bit. Includes the StellaCordis VST3 instrument for compatible VST3 DAW hosts.

Download PC package

Copy StellaCordis.vst3 to C:\Program Files\Common Files\VST3\, then rescan plugins in your DAW. Look under VST3 > RenaissanceFieldLite > StellaCordis.
